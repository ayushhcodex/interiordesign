Our Story

Born From a Passion
for Beautiful Spaces

Founded in 2015 by Aria Lumina, our studio has grown from a one-person vision into one of India's most celebrated interior design practices.

The Beginning

A Studio Built on Craft

Lumina was founded with a singular belief: that great interior design is not a luxury for the few, but a transformative force that should be accessible to anyone who believes their environment shapes their life.

Our Principal Designer, Jay Shaha, studied at the National Institute of Design, grounding her practice in strong design fundamentals and material intelligence. After years of hands-on experience in furniture manufacturing, he transitioned into interior design with a deep understanding of craftsmanship and functionality.

Today, our 50+ person team handles everything from every type of furniture manuufacturing to interior design and renovations to large-scale commercial developments with the same meticulous attention to detail and commitment to the client's vision.
