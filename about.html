The Next Chapter of a Trusted Legacy

From the Makers of Ambika Furniture
to Ratnakala Interiors.

At Ratnakala Interiors, we don’t just decorate rooms; we build them from the ground up. We are the specialized design branch of Ambika Furniture—a name that Davangere has trusted for many years for quality and honest work. We believe that great design should not be a luxury for just a few, but a way to make every home and office more comfortable, functional, and beautiful.

Our Story

A Foundation of Quality

For decades, Ambika Furniture has been known across Karnataka for making strong, long-lasting furniture. But we realized that a beautiful house needs more than just good furniture—it needs a complete vision and a professional touch.

Ratnakala Interiors was started to bring together the best of both worlds: the creative ideas of a modern design studio and the technical skills of a real furniture factory.

Our team carries a strong design background from the National Institute of Design (NID) and years of hands-on experience in our own manufacturing units. We have moved from simply making furniture to designing entire living and working environments.

Why Choose Us?

The Ratnakala Edge

Most designers only give you drawings and then hire other people to do the work. At Ratnakala, we do the work ourselves. Because we are backed by the Ambika Furniture factory, our clients get several benefits:

✦

Knowledge of Materials

We don’t just pick wood or stone; we understand how they work and how long they will last. We know which materials work best in our local climate so your home stays beautiful for years to come.

◎

Direct from the Factory

Because we make our own furniture and cabinets in our own workshop, we can control the quality perfectly. There is no middleman, which means better quality and better value for you.

◈

A Large, Expert Team

With a team of over 50 skilled professionals, we can handle everything—from small home renovations to large commercial projects—with the same care and attention to detail.

Our Philosophy

"Design should be both smart and beautiful.
At Ratnakala, we believe a room should be as strong as a piece of Ambika furniture and as breathtaking as a work of art."

Rooted in Davangere

While our designs look modern and fresh, our heart is in Davangere. We understand our local culture, our traditions, and how families here like to live.

Ratnakala is not just a new business; it is the next generation of a family legacy.
